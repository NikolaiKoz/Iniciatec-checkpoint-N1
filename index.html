What is Iniciatec?

IniciaTec is a training program, sponsored by Accenture Argentina, whose objective is the development of technical and socio-emotional skills aligned to the digital economy working together with Eidos and MindHub.

Also empowering passionate people in an environment that emulates an agile, innovative and collaborative system. Providing grat skills for the current work environment.

What technologies do We use?

We use technologies for Front-End Web development with the MERN (MongoDB, ExpressJS, React, NodeJS) JavaScript stack, we also learn about Testing and Monitoring Services to carry out end-to-end web services.

What methodology do We implement?

We implement in a practical way the SCRUM methodology with Sprints and daily meetings through the philosophy "Learning by doing". On the other hand we develop skills for the current work culture, collaborative and flexible teamwork to face the labor market
