Flow GAMERGY Argentina 2022

Where?

The largest video game and electronic sports (esports) festival in Spain, Flow Gamergy, will display its face-to-face event in Argentina for the first time and expect to attract more than 23 thousand fans. As part of the proposal, attendees will enjoy the final phases of several tournaments and the definition of the "Copa Libertadores" of League of Legends (LoL).

Argentina Game Show Flow 2022

Where?

AGS is the most important video game and technology festival in Argentina. It will feature exclusive launches, numerous exhibitors, Esports tournaments, a cosplay contest, live shows, talks and workshops, a space for the national industry, streamers and content creators and much more.

FiReLEAGUE Argentina

Where?

The FiReLEAGUE Argentina of CSGO 2022 will live this week in great definition and will deliver classifications to different tournaments, in addition to 3 million pesos in prizes. The playoffs will be played in person, between August 4 and 7, at the Palermo gaming center.

Valorant Champions Tour

Where?
